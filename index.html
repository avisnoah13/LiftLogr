How It Works

LiftTrack uses advanced custom electronics embedded directly into gym machines, barbells, weights, and dumbbells to automatically detect every lift and the exact weight you’re using — without you needing to press any buttons or manually log anything.

These smart sensors continuously capture your workout data and securely send it to the cloud in real-time. From there, our platform organizes and visualizes your lifting progress with intuitive charts, performance summaries, and personalized insights — all accessible anytime on your phone or computer.

Focus on crushing your goals while we take care of the tracking.
